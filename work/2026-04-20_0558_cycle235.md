## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization using a random method (alphabetical, first 5). I will read their primary documentation files (README.md, any CONTRIBUTING.md, etc.) and score them based on the fleet's standard for useful, factual, tile-generating documentation.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**
*   **0-3:** Missing, severely outdated, or hallucinated content. No practical use.
*   **4-6:** Basic structure exists but is incomplete, vague, or lacks concrete examples. Fails the "hermit crab" test (cannot move in and start work).
*   **7-8:** Clear purpose, setup instructions, and core API/usage. A contributor can understand and begin work. May lack advanced context or integration notes.
*   **9-10:** Exemplary. Includes vision context, clear examples, contribution guidelines, architecture diagrams, and explicit connections to other fleet components (P0/P1/P2 thinking evident).
