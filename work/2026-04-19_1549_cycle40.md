## Step 2: Documentation Quality Assessment

I will read each repository's primary documentation (README.md) and any other top-level docs. The scoring rubric (0-10) is:
*   **0-3:** Missing, severely outdated, or completely unhelpful.
*   **4-6:** Basic information present but incomplete, unclear, or lacks critical details (e.g., setup, purpose).
*   **7-8:** Good. Clearly states purpose, setup, and usage. Could be improved with examples, architecture diagrams, or contribution guides.
*   **9-10:** Excellent. Comprehensive, clear, includes examples, diagrams, API references, and contribution guidelines.
