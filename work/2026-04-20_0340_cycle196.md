## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization. I will read their primary documentation files (README.md, any additional docs) and score them based on a standard rubric.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**
*   **0-3 (Poor):** Missing, incomplete, or severely outdated. No clear purpose or usage instructions.

*   **7-8 (Good):** Clear purpose, setup, and usage. Includes examples and links to related resources.
*   **9-10 (Excellent):** Comprehensive, well-structured, and includes vision, architecture, examples, contribution guidelines, and clear navigation.
