## Step 1: Repository Selection & Scoring

I will select 5 repositories from the fleet's known list and evaluate their primary documentation (README.md) based on the following criteria derived from fleet standards:
1.  **Clarity of Purpose (P0):** Does it state what the project is and why it exists?
2.  **Technical Prerequisites (P1):** Does it list required tools, versions, and dependencies?
3.  **Getting Started (P1):** Are there clear, executable steps to run/install?
4.  **Architecture/Usage (P2):** Does it explain key components, APIs, or how to use it?
5.  **Contribution Links (P2):** Does it link to related projects, issues, or contribution guides?

**Scoring:** 0-2 points per criterion (0=missing, 1=partial, 2=clear). Total out of 10.
