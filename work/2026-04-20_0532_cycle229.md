## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization. I will read their primary documentation (README.md) and any other relevant docs, then score them based on clarity, completeness, and usefulness for a new contributor.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**

## Step 2: Summary Report

**Overall Fleet Documentation Health: Mixed.**

*   **Strengths:** Foundational projects like `plato-torch` and libraries like `constraint-theory-core` have excellent, contributor-ready documentation. They successfully communicate purpose and mechanics.
*   **Weaknesses:** Mid-level infrastructure and simulation projects (`holodeck-rust`, `fleet-simulator`, `cudaclaw`) suffer from sparse, context-poor documentation. They assume significant prior knowledge of the fleet's architecture and goals.
