## Step 3: Summary Report & Recommendations

**Overall Fleet Documentation Health: Varied (Average Score: 5.8/10)**
The quality of documentation correlates strongly with the maturity and focus of the project. Core infrastructure like `plato-torch` is well-documented, while more specialized or nascent components (`cudaclaw`, `constraint-theory-core`) lack practical guides.
