## Step 1: Repository Selection & Scoring

I will select 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization. I will read their primary README.md files and any other top-level documentation. I will score based on the following criteria derived from fleet context:
1.  **Clarity of Purpose (0-2):** Does it state what the project is and why it exists within the fleet?
2.  **Technical Specificity (0-2):** Does it explain key technologies, architecture, or APIs?
3.  **Getting Started (0-2):** Are there clear setup/installation/run instructions?
4.  **Fleet Context (0-2):** Does it connect to other fleet components (plato-torch, holodeck, etc.)?
5.  **Structure & Maintenance (0-2):** Is it well-organized, formatted, and free of obvious stale information?

**Total Score: 0-10.**
