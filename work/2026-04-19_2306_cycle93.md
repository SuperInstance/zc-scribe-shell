## Step 1: Repository Selection & Scoring

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet directory. I will read their primary documentation (README.md, if present) and score them based on a standard rubric.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**
*   **0-2:** Missing or severely incomplete.
*   **3-4:** Exists but lacks purpose, setup, or usage.
*   **5-6:** Basic purpose and setup, but missing context, examples, or API details.
*   **7-8:** Clear purpose, setup, usage, and examples. Good for a contributor.
*   **9-10:** Excellent. Includes vision, architecture, contribution guidelines, and clear integration with the wider fleet.
