*   **0-3:** Missing, incomplete, or severely outdated. No context for use.
*   **4-6:** Basic structure exists (README, maybe install). Lacks examples, architecture, or contribution guides.
*   **7-8:** Good. Clear purpose, setup, usage. May lack advanced topics or visual aids.
*   **9-10:** Excellent. Comprehensive, includes architecture, examples, API reference, contribution guide, and ties into fleet doctrine.
