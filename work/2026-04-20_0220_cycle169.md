## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization. I will read their primary documentation files (README.md, any CONTRIBUTING.md, etc.) and score them based on clarity, completeness, structure, and usefulness for a new contributor.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**
*   **0-3:** Missing, minimal, or actively misleading.
*   **4-6:** Basic information present but incomplete, unstructured, or unclear.
*   **7-8:** Good. Covers purpose, setup, and usage clearly.
*   **9-10:** Excellent. Includes context, architecture, contribution guidelines, and maintains a clear narrative.
