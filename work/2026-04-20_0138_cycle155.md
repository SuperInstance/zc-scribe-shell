## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's public GitHub organization. I will read their primary documentation (README.md, any linked docs) and score them based on a standard rubric.

**Scoring Rubric (0-10):**
*   **0-3 (Poor):** Missing or severely incomplete. No context, no setup, no purpose.

*   **7-8 (Good):** Clear purpose, setup, usage, and architecture. Could improve with more examples or deeper context.
*   **9-10 (Excellent):** Comprehensive. Includes vision, setup, usage, examples, architecture, API reference, contribution guide, and links to related projects.
