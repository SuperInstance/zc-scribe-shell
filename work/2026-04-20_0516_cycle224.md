## Step 1: Repository Selection & Analysis

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet's GitHub organization. I will read their primary documentation files (README.md, any additional docs) and score them based on the following criteria derived from fleet doctrine and common best practices:

*   **Clarity (0-2):** Is the purpose and scope immediately clear?
