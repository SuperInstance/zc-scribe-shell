## Step 1: Repository Selection & Scoring

I have selected 5 repositories from the fleet directory. I will read their primary documentation (README.md, any other top-level docs) and score them based on the following criteria derived from fleet context:
1.  **Clarity of Purpose (P0):** Does it clearly state what it is and what it is NOT for?
2.  **Technical Accuracy (P1):** Are commands, APIs, and dependencies correct?
3.  **Onboarding Path (P2):** Does it guide a new contributor from zero to a useful contribution?
4.  **Fleet Context:** Does it connect itself to other fleet components (plato-torch, holodeck, etc.)?

**Scoring Key:** 0-2 (Missing/Critical), 3-5 (Poor/Incomplete), 6-7 (Adequate), 8-9 (Good), 10 (Exemplary/Fleet Standard).
