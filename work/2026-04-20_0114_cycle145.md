## Step 3: Summary Report

**Overall Fleet Documentation Health: Suboptimal (Average Score: 5.2/10)**

**Findings:**
1.  **Inconsistent Depth:** Documentation quality varies widely, from good (`plato-torch`) to very poor (`cudaclaw`, `constraint-theory-core`).
